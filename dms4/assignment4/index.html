Echoscape

Final Project Reflection and Analysis

Judgements of Purpose

The main objective of Echoscape was to develop an interactive digital soundscape that invites users to playfully and spatially explore and engage with audio. In addition to being a creative tool, I wanted the project to be a meditative experience where users could immerse themselves in sound and try out various aesthetics with changing background colors and audio components. My ultimate goal was for Echoscape to encourage curiosity, offer a feeling of exploration, and provide a relaxing multisensory experience instead of just passive listening.

Judgements of Worth

Beyond novelty, I prioritized accessibility and emotional impact. In order to ensure that users of all ages and technical skill levels could enjoy Echoscape, it was important that it is accessible for everyone and that no prior experience is needed. I also wanted the project to evoke a sense of calmness, relaxation, and wonder, providing users with a gentle escape. Accessibility, ease of use, and emotional impact were all the core values that guided the project's development.

Judgements of Framing

To prevent overwhelming users with too many options, I intentionally kept the design space to simple functionality and a minimalist aesthetic while still incorporating ambient audio. This decision was made to maximize accessibility and ensure a welcoming experience for everyone. Conceptually, I aimed to create ambient, peaceful soundscapes using echo in tone.js, avoiding loud or overly complicated audio elements to maintain a consistent and calming environment.

Judgements of Appearance

For the visual style, I selected a minimalist and calming aesthetic, using a soft color palette and fluid animations. The intention was to create a visually soothing environment based on the themes that complemented the ambient audio. I mainly focused on using gradients for the relaxing background and growing shapes to match the echo audio. In addition, I included several themes to provide users with a variety of spaces that they may enjoy. I had originally intended to create a blooming flower shape in the garden theme. However, due to time constraints, I decided to use the ellipse shape instead, as it appeared to represent a petal or leaf-like shape.

Judgements of Quality

There were several decisions and alterations I made regarding the technical aspects of the project. I used Konva.js for the visuals, including the creation and animation of shapes, and Tone.js for the audio functionality. I adjusted the timing of the growing circles to be longer so they would better match the audio. For the sound, I originally used the built-in synth from Tone.js, but it didnâ€™t sound like what I was aiming for. I then sourced some audio samples from Pixabay, which I implemented with Tone.js, allowing me to pitch the notes up or down as needed. To give users more options to explore, I also repositioned the notes from the Y axis to the X axis.

Judgements of Composition

When creating echoscape, I stick with a minimalistic and relaxing layout and experience. I carefully balanced each element, creating a multisensory experience with calming visuals and soothing audio. This balance was achieved by designing intuitive controls that invite experimentation without overwhelming the user. The end result is a project that feels cohesive and thoughtfully designed, with each component contributing to the overall atmosphere. Nevertheless, one element didn't function as planned. At one point, I decided to reduce the background volume so that the instrument notes weren't overpowered and everything was balanced. However, it turned out that it was too quiet, and users could no longer hear it clearly. I will make the necessary adjustments and incorporate them into future changes.

Judgements of Navigation

During the project's development, I prioritized the simple layout, graphics, and audio elements before adding other features like the volume and theme picker. I also made sure to design a straightforward and user-friendly interface for the users, ensuring that they do not feel overwhelmed and instead experience the sense of calm and tranquility that I am aiming for. Once all of the essential features were functioning correctly, I focused on experimenting with colors and added additional components to my project.
