SOFTWARE OVERVIEW

1. What genre of creative software do you believe it belongs to?

Wigglypaint falls into the genre of digital painter, allowing users to express themselves through digital art with the provided features.


2. List three other examples of software in this genre

  • Shake Art
  • Kidpix
  • MS Paint

    • FEATURES AND MECHANIC

    3. List the program's interactive features/mechanics

  • Drawing Tools : A set of brushes for creating colorful, wobbly lines and shapes
  • Eraser Tool : Remove or refine unwanted marks
  • Obliterate : Restarts the whole canvas
  • Marker Size : Adjust the size of the stroke
  • 'Oops' button : Undo or redo the previous drawing or stroke
  • Colour Palette : Change to a desirable colour palette with a variety of choices for users to select or input users own preferred colours
  • Crop : Adjust and crops canvas to desired size
  • Export : Saves artwork as image or GIF
  • Transparency Mask : Create partially transparent animation in GIF
  • Widget : Move around elements in the layout
  • Decker : Adjust or modify the program script


    • 4. What features/mechanics indicate its genre by aligning with its conventions?

    Wigglypaint includes drawing tools with a range of brushes, eraser tools, color palettes, canvas clearing, and the option to export and crop the canvas, all of which align with digital painter conventions.


    5. What features/mechanics are novel for its genre?

    Wigglypaint adds new elements to the digital painting genre, such as interactive sound feedback and a wiggly animation effect, which makes it more engaging and kid-friendly. Additionally, the integration of Decker in Wigglypaint also allows users to access and modify the code and design a whole new layout, where users can program their own interactive platform using the provided code and tools, which encourages experimentation.


    6. What features/mechanics common to its genre are missing or might be added?

    Looking through the features provided, there are several standard elements that are lacking, including layers and zoom capabilities. Ideally, a larger option of color palettes and an additional set of brushes could also be added to increase creative possibilities.

    USER EXPERIENCE

    7. What context, community or niche is it primarily aimed at and what indicates this?

    The target audience for Wigglypaint appears to be artistic individuals who enjoy creating digital art in a casual and playful medium, allowing them to express their unique style, particularly those who love quirky doodles and fanart. Wigglypaint's playful interface, user-friendly design, and special features like wiggly animations and sound feedback make it enjoyable and approachable for a diverse range of users, from beginners to experienced artists.


    8. What did you find annoying and/or boring about interacting with it?

    Due to its limited tools provided, Wigglypaint can become slightly restrictive, and it felt quite repetitive over some time, which might not be as engaging. Furthermore, even though we can turn the sound off, it can be somewhat annoying after a while.


    9. What did you find enjoyable about interacting with it?

    The simplicity and straightforwardness of the program allow me to create random and fun doodles with no thought given to it. The animation of the wiggly lines and sound feedback also make it more engaging and enjoyable when I first interact with it.
