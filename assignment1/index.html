First Impression

1. What was the first thing you paid attention to when interacting with the experience?

The first thing that caught my attention was the 3D graphics, which highlighted different sections of the interface. When I hovered over these graphics, they responded to my mouse movements, where several elements moved, and popup text followed my cursor. This made it clear that certain parts of the interface were interactive. I then noticed other touchable elements that reacted to my mouse, with a circle showing my movements getting larger, indicating that more elements were interactable. After this, I saw a prompt that said, "Scroll to explore the Luniverse" at the bottom of the homepage. When I scrolled, the 3D elements began floating, leading me to the next page. These interactive 3D elements were key in drawing me into the experience. Additionally, the minimalist design, clean layout, and calming color scheme of the homepage also stood out, making the interface both engaging and visually appealing.
